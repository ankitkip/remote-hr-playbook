# Pre-Joining Checklist (Remote Employees)

The employee experience starts **before Day 1**.

## One Week Before Joining
- Send welcome email with:
  - Joining date & time
  - Reporting manager
  - First-day agenda
- Share:
  - Offer letter
  - Company handbook
  - IT & security guidelines
